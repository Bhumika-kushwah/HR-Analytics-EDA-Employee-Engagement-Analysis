# 📊 HR Analytics EDA – Employee Engagement Analysis

## 🔍 Project Overview
This project focuses on performing Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on an HR dataset to uncover key insights related to employee performance, engagement, salary distribution, and attrition.

The goal is to transform employee data into actionable insights that can help HR teams make data-driven decisions.

---

## 📁 Dataset Information
- Source: Kaggle (HRDataset_v14)  
- Records: 311 employees  
- Features: 36 columns  

### Key Columns:
- Employee Name  
- Department  
- Position  
- Salary  
- Performance Score  
- Employee Satisfaction  
- Absences  
- Recruitment Source  
- Termination Reason  
- Engagement Survey  
- Gender  

- Data Type: Mixed (Numerical + Categorical)  
- Nature: Real-world dataset with missing values and inconsistencies  

---

## 🧹 Data Cleaning & Preprocessing

- Handled missing values (filled appropriately)  
- Removed duplicate records  
- Verified dataset structure (311 rows × 36 columns)  
- Standardized numerical and categorical data  
- Validated important columns (Salary, Absences, Engagement, etc.)  

💡 Note: Proper handling of missing values (mean/median/mode) is critical for reliable analysis  

---

## 📊 Exploratory Data Analysis

### 🔹 Univariate Analysis
- Salary distribution  
- Performance score distribution  
- Employee satisfaction levels  

### 🔹 Bivariate Analysis
- Salary by department  
- Satisfaction vs attrition  
- Performance vs engagement  

### 🔹 Multivariate Analysis
- Combined analysis of salary, performance, and engagement  
- Department-wise patterns in satisfaction and attrition  

---

## 📌 Key Insights

### 💰 Salary Insights
- 5.5× gap between highest and lowest salaries  
- High salary variation at top levels  

### 📉 Performance Insights
- 78% employees meet expectations  
- 13 employees are on Performance Improvement Plan (PIP) → requires immediate HR attention  

### 🌐 Recruitment Insights
- 70%+ hiring comes from digital platforms (Indeed, LinkedIn, Google)  
- Digital channels dominate hiring strategy  

### 😊 Engagement & Satisfaction
- 33% employees show low satisfaction (ratings 1–2)  
- Indicates potential retention risk  

### 💼 Department Insights
- Executive department: highest salary & engagement  
- Production department: lowest salary & engagement  

### 🚪 Attrition Insights
- Top reason for leaving: Better job opportunities  
- Suggests need for improved compensation and career growth  

### 🚻 Gender Insights
- Male median salary is ~$4K higher than female  
- Indicates a potential gender pay gap  

---

## 💡 Key Learnings

- Data cleaning is not just a step — it's the foundation  
- Incorrect datatypes can mislead analysis  
- Proper preprocessing is critical before EDA  
- EDA is not just visualization — it’s about solving real business problems  
- Data-driven insights can directly support HR decision-making
